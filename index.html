Connecting to Kirby

  • Host: kirby.webreakstuff.com
  • Port: 6667 (normal) and 6697 (SSL)
  • Known channels: #startups, #wbs

Recommended clients

  • Mac: Limechat, Colloquy, irssi with homebrew
  • Linux: irssi, XChat, BitchX
  • Windows: mIRC

Other notes

  • Our recommended setup, for the curious is Limechat + these themes and Adobe's Source Code Pro.
  • We use freenode's ircd-seven and Atheme services. Most freenode commands should work here, which means their help documents apply.
  • Be helpful. Be as mature as the IRC protocol ;)
